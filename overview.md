## 💡 The Idea

Most university innovation ecosystems are **fragmented**. Students are building projects, alumni are looking to give back, and investors are looking for the next big idea—but they aren't talking to each other. Information is stuck in WhatsApp groups and spreadsheets.

**INNOVEX** is a unified digital intelligence platform that acts as the "Operating System" for campus innovation. It doesn't just list projects; it uses **Explainable AI** to understand the skills, goals, and potential of every user to create high-impact matches.

---

## 🌍 Why it Matters (The Problem)

- **Invisible Progress:** Student achievements aren't tracked centrally, leading to weak portfolios.
- **Random Mentorship:** Finding a mentor is currently based on luck, not compatibility.
- **Disconnected Alumni:** Institutions lose touch with successful graduates who could be key investors.
- **No Data:** Colleges have no "Innovation Scorecard" to show their real-world impact to ranking bodies like NIRF.

---

## 🛠 Core Features

### 1. Unified Dashboards

- **Students:** Track "Innovation Scores," build digital portfolios, and complete daily missions.
- **Mentors:** Get suggested startups to guide based on a compatibility score.
- **Investors:** View a vetted pipeline of campus startups from "Idea" to "Scaling."
- **Admins:** Access real-time analytics on the entire ecosystem’s health and growth.

### 2. Explainable AI Matchmaking

Instead of "Black Box" suggestions, our engine tells you **why** you were matched.

- _Example:_ "Matched with Mentor Rahul because you both focus on SDG 9 (Infrastructure) and he has 5+ years in FinTech."

### 3. Gamification & Dynamic Avatars

To keep engagement high, INNOVEX uses an evolution system:

- **Avatar States:** Your avatar changes based on your "Innovation Score" and activity streaks.
- **Emotions:** Avatars react to your progress (e.g., "Excited" when you win a hackathon).
- **Badges:** Unlock visual upgrades for your profile by hitting major milestones.

### 4. The RS ID System

Every user gets a unique **RS ID**—a permanent digital identity that follows a student from their first year of college into their professional life as a mentor or investor.

### 5. One-Click Social Sync

Instantly share your milestones. One button post-notifies your achievements to **LinkedIn** and **WhatsApp**, ensuring your professional network stays updated.

## 🏗 The Tech Stack (2026 Edition)

- **Frontend:** Next.js 16 (React Compiler Enabled) + Tailwind CSS 4.0
- **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL + Real-time)
- **AI Engine:** Groq (Llama 3.3 70B) for blazing-fast reasoning.
- **Vector Search:** Supabase pgvector for skill-based matchmaking.
- **Animations:** dotLottie-web for state-based avatars.
